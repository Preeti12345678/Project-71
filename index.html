It was open to students from 10th to 12th and they fiercely argued by every team who started their case with high degree of insight and awareness. What's new

  • New Playground!

    They made a new park in the school near the front gate! We can play there in break time,P.T preiod and if we're waiting for our parents to come pick us up! I love to play in the park.

  • New Attendance Scanner!

    We now have a attendance scanner device from which teachers will not have to mark the attendance manully. The students have to scan their Icard when they enter the classroom which will send a message to the parents and will mark their attendance. The same device has been put in buses, when the students come into the bus the parents will be sent a message that their child is safe and sound in the bus and same will happen when they drop the child home.
